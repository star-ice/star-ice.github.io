反爬虫技术--Headers

Posted on October 16, 2018

什么是爬虫和反爬虫

####这里引用携程对其的定义

  • 爬虫：使用任何技术手段，批量获取网站信息的一种方式。关键在于批量。
  • 反爬虫：使用任何技术手段，阻止别人批量获取自己网站信息的一种方式。关键也在于批量。

####这里是一些其他的相关术语的定义

  • 误伤：在反爬虫的过程中，错误的将普通用户识别为爬虫。误伤率高的反爬虫策略，效果再好也不能用。
  • 拦截：成功地阻止爬虫访问。这里会有拦截率的概念。通常来说，拦截率越高的反爬虫策略，误伤的可能性就越高。因此需要做个权衡。
  • 资源：机器成本与人力成本的总和。

图解用户访问请求网站的过程

在建立TCP连接之后，客户端就会发送HTTP请求报文，这里也同样包括了HTTP Headers的信息，之后服务器端会对HTTP请求报文进行处理，这里就会经过服务器目标网站的爬虫检测机制（对 HTTP Headers 进行校验），如果验证通过，则正常返回目标网站内容

Headers反爬虫技术

#####1. 服务器端可以根据 HTTP Headers 中的 User-Agent^1 来检查客户端是否是一个合法的浏览器程序，即获得同频（浏览器加载这个页面的完整文档）

#####2. 服务器也可以利用浏览器 HTTP Headers 指纹[^2]，根据客户端声明的浏览器的厂商以及版本（来自 User-Agent ），鉴别出 Headers 中的各字段是否符合该浏览器的特征

#####3. 服务器端可以对所有访问页面的 HTTP 请求在 HTTP response 中种下一个 cookie token ，然后在这个页面内异步执行的一些 Ajax 接口里去校验来访请求是否会有该 cookie token ，如果有则表明是一个合法的浏览器来访

######ps：页面的所有核心内容都可以做成 JS 异步请求 Ajax 获取数据后渲染在页面中，增加爬虫爬取的难度

[^2]: 比如硬件类型（Apple）、操作系统（Mac OS）、用户代理（User agent）、系统字体、语言、屏幕分辨率、浏览器插件 (Flash, Silverlight, Java, etc)、浏览器扩展、浏览器设置 (Do-Not-Track, etc)、时区差（Browser GMT Offset）等众多信息，可以辅助识别浏览器信息

DO YOU KNOW?

Most people in China claimed that Writing is one of the most difficult parts in IELTS, another part is Speaking. IELTS Writing has a lot of requirements for examinees. Examinees are not only required to be logical but also to write consistently and accurately.

IELTS Writing have 4 requests

1.Task response
     Read writing question carefully and Be sure that you understand it. You have to comprehend what the question focus on and what contents that your answer should include.
2.Coherence & cohesion
     This means your essay ought to have a logical structure. If you point out an opinion, you must give out supporting point so that your opinion will not be weak and if necessary, give out some examples. Besides, a good link is vital between one opinion to another. Your essay should not be separate.
3.Lexical resource(accurate & approperate)
     Some words such as “good”, “kind” ,”think” appearance frequently in Chinese examinees’ essaies. It’s not a positive performance for one’s Lexical resource. Try to replace those words to an advanced vocabulary. If you are rich in lexical resource, you must not use any advanced word in your essay. Try to use them appropriately.
4.Grammatical range & accuracy
     Use independent sentence, dependent sentence, compound sentence, complex sentence and conjunctive adverb correctly.

Conjunctive adverbs & Coordinate conjunctions & Subordinating conjuctions

1.What is conjunctive adverbs?

Conjunctive adverbs (also called linking adverbs or connecting adverbs) are a specific type of conjunction. Conjunctions are used to join together words, phrases, or clauses. Conjunctive adverbs are specifically used to connect two independent clauses.

Conjunctive adverbs show the when, where, why, or how of a verb, adjective, or adverb.Some conjunctive adverbs are also used as coordinating conjunctions, that it not adverbially but to coordinate two independent clauses; they are for, whereas, nor, yet, so

2.What is coordinate conjunctions?

Coordinating conjunctions are used to link two clauses or phrases of equal value or equal status.
There are only a small number of coordinating conjunctions in English: most sources repeat what others say, and list the following seven, using the convenient acronym FANBOYS.
for, and, nor, but, or, yet and so.
It is perhaps preferable to exclude for and so, and prefer the acronym BANYO.

3.What is subordinating conjunctions?

Subordinating conjunctions are used to link two clauses within a single sentence, when one clause is subordinate to the other.
In other words, the subordinate clause clarifies, expands or explains the meaning of the main clause.

Some types of subordinate clause are introduced by subordinating conjunctions, others (such as relative clauses) are not. Common subordinating conjunctions include

  • as , because and since (cause)
  • so and so that (purpose)
  • although and though (contrastive)
  • after, before, until, while, etc. (temporal)
  • if, unless, as long as, provided, whenever, whatever (conditional, indirect question)
  • that (reported speech, indirect statement, consequential)

Tips of “So”:

So is a subordinating conjunction when it is used to denote a purpose. A so clause denoting purpose does not usually come before the main clause, but it is not impossible (example 6).
When so is used with the meaning of therefore or and similarly , it is a conjunctive adverb.
